To You who Struggle with Meal Planning:

We know that it is super hard to find the most fitting meal plan for you.
You have dietary restrictions and goals that keep changing.
And you want to find the meals that have all your favorite food in it?
Don't worry! We understand that and we are here to help!


This website will guide you from not knowing what to cook to having a grocery list ready to go!
It has three main steps to find the right meal for you.

Step 1:
Generate 10 preliminary meals based on your dietary restrictions and goals
Step 2:
You choose the meal you are most interested in and personalize the recipe
Step 3:
Generate the grocery list that is ready to go!

To start, press on the Find a Meal Button on the top of the screen!
